A look at Dreamshop business process model for their Ecommerce platform.

by Whznext on April 28 2015 In Business IT

The purpose of this business model is to get the impression of how various actors will be engaged on Dreamshop marketplace (client) and get to understand the services that the Dreamshop web service (server) will be offering. This will help us draw a system boundary and identify major use cases.

Business Model of Dreamshop

Dreamshop web service is a software system designed to support buying, selling and marketing of goods and services offered by East Electrical Ventures and partners on platforms such as web, mobile, etc. East Electrical Ventures presented a business model and we identified various points an actor begin various processes and how they end those processes when they are using the service. We mapped relationships between actors, systems within Dreamshop marketplace and Dreamshop web service to create a common understanding of the content of the business process, results and a baseline to which improvements can be suggested by various stakeholders.
